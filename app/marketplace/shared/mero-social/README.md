# Mero Social

A comprehensive Social Media Management (SMM) application integrated into the Mero Jugx platform.

## Overview
Mero Social enables organizations and creators to manage their social media presence across multiple platforms from a single unified interface.

## Features (Planned)
- **Unified Inbox**: Manage comments and DMs from all platforms
- **Content Calendar**: Drag-and-drop scheduling
- **AI Composer**: Generate captions and hashtags
- **Analytics Hub**: Combined growth metrics
- **Brand Workspaces**: Separate clients/brands

## Usage
Access via the Mero Jugx marketplace or your organization dashboard.
